How to Use the Flooring Calculator

Using this tool is straightforward. Follow these steps to get an accurate estimate for your project:

  • ● Measure the Room: Use a tape measure to find the length and width of the floor area you plan to cover.
  • ● Input Dimensions: Enter these numbers into the "Length" and "Width" fields in the calculator.
  • ● Add Waste Factor (Optional): It is standard practice to add 10% to 15% extra for cutting, errors, and future repairs. Most calculators have a "Waste" percentage field for this.
  • ● Calculate: Click the "Calculate" button to see the total square footage and the number of flooring boxes required.

The Formula

To find the area of a rectangular room, we use a simple geometric formula:

Area = Length x times Width

If you are accounting for waste, the formula adjusts to:

Total Square Footage = (Length x times Width) x times (1 + Waste Percentage)
