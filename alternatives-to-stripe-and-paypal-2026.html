💔 Why merchants are leaving Stripe & PayPal (real problems)

💰

Hidden fees add up fast

PayPal charges up to 4.4% + fixed fee for international transactions. Stripe adds a 1% currency conversion markup plus 1.5% for international cards. On $50k monthly revenue, that’s over $2,000 wasted.

🚫

Account freezes & holds

PayPal is notorious for freezing accounts for 180 days with no warning. Stripe can also hold up to 30% of your rolling reserve for “risk”. These freezes kill cash flow for small businesses.

🌍

Poor multi-currency support

Neither Stripe nor PayPal give you local receiving accounts in foreign currencies. You pay FX fees twice — when you receive and when you convert. Modern alternatives like Airwallex offer 23+ local accounts with zero hidden margins.

⚖️

Expensive dispute fees

Stripe charges $15 per chargeback even if you win. PayPal charges $20 non-refundable dispute fee. For high-volume merchants, that adds thousands in unnecessary costs.
