How Adding and Subtracting Fractions Calculator works?

  • Add or subtract: convert both fractions to a common denominator first.
  • Multiply: multiply the numerators and denominators directly.
  • Divide: multiply the first fraction by the reciprocal of the second.

Why simplified fractions matter

A simplified fraction is easier to read and compare. For example, 6/8 is correct, but 3/4 is the reduced form most teachers and textbooks expect. By simplifying automatically, the calculator saves time and reduces mistakes from manual reduction.
