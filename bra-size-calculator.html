Find your estimated bra size online

This bra size calculator is built to give a quick starting point using two measurements: underbust and full bust. Many people search for terms like measure bra size, find my bra size, or bra size calculator usa because sizing can vary by brand and style. A calculator does not replace a fitting room, but it can reduce guesswork and help narrow down the most likely band and cup size.

The page works best when measurements are taken carefully with a soft measuring tape. Measure the underbust snugly around the ribcage and the full bust at the fullest point. From there, the tool estimates a band size and then uses the difference between bust and band to suggest the cup size.

How bra sizing works

  • Band size is based on the ribcage measurement.
  • Cup size is based on the difference between the bust and the band.
  • Brand fit, fabric stretch, and style can still change the final fit.

Why this calculator is useful

A quick online calculator is helpful before shopping, ordering from a new brand, or checking whether your current size still makes sense. It is especially useful if you want a starting point before trying multiple sizes manually.

FAQ

Is the result exact?No. It is an estimate and should be treated as a starting point because brands and bra styles fit differently.
What if I am between sizes?Try the nearest size and a sister size if needed. Small differences in band and cup can change comfort a lot.
Can I use this calculator at home?Yes. It is designed for simple at-home use with a measuring tape and two measurements.
