How to use this calculator

Enter your weight, height, age, and gender in the input fields.


Next, choose your preferred unit (Metric or Imperial) if needed. Click the Calculate BMI & Analysis button to instantly get the result.


The calculator will show:

● Your BMI value


● Your BMI category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese)


● A personalized analysis based on your age and gender


Make sure all values are valid and greater than zero to avoid errors.

Formula used

To calculate BMI, the calculator follows this formula:


BMI = Weight ÷ Height²


● In Metric: weight (kg) ÷ height (m²)


● In Imperial: weight (lbs) ÷ height (in²) × 703


The result is then interpreted based on:


● Standard BMI ranges (adults)


● Age-specific guidance (children & seniors)


● Gender-based insights


FAQ

1. Can I use this tool for children and teens?


Yes, but for users under 18, BMI is interpreted using age and gender percentiles, not just the number.


2. Is this calculator accurate for all body types?


BMI is a general health indicator. It does not consider muscle mass or body composition, so athletes may get slightly misleading results.


3. How does this BMI calculator work?


It calculates BMI using your height and weight, then analyzes it using age and gender to give a more personalized health interpretation.


4. Can I use this calculator for weight loss planning?


Yes, it gives a good starting point to understand your current health status, but for detailed planning, consult a professional.


5. Why are age and gender important in BMI?


Because body composition changes with age and differs between males and females, making the result more meaningful when these factors are included.
